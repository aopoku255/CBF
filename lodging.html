FORUM LODGING

The 2024 Supply Chain Business will be held at the World Trade Center, Accra, Ghana

Nestled in the city centre is the business address of choice Alisa Hotel – North Ridge. Located just 6km from Kotoka International Airport, offers luxury accommodation with a multiplicity of standard rooms and suite room types

The hotel is equipped with over 14 plenary and syndicate conference rooms as well as an imposing ball room to host receptions, exhibitions or any memorable event.

The hotel has a health club which consists of a fully equipped gymnasium, tennis court, swimming pool, a salon and spa.
